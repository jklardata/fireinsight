How It Works

From data to report
in three steps

No training required. If you can fill out a form, you can use FireInsight.

1

Connect Your Data

Enter your NERIS department ID and select a date range. Or use our sample data to see FireInsight in action immediately—no credentials needed.

2

Choose a Report Type

Select from Incident Trends, Chief's Monthly Report, or Grant Narrative. For grants, pick your program (AFG or SAFER) and describe what you're requesting.

3

Generate and Export

Click Generate. Claude AI analyzes your incident data and produces a complete, ready-to-use document in under 30 seconds. Download as Markdown or copy to clipboard.
